Continuous Delivery

I help organisations and teams achieve a state of continuous delivery, i.e. being able to release product increments sufficiently reliably and quickly to satisfy customer demand.

It is first and foremost a set of principles and practices to adopt. These will help teams deliver significantly faster at a sustainable pace, with higher quality and lower costs. This involves both technological and organisational changes.

Continuous Delivery is not a tooling problem. The tooling is the easy part to solve.
