[But, compliance!]({% post_url 2022-02-22-on-the-evilness-of-feature-branching-but-compliance %}) Somehow a welcome justification for all the gates in their software delivery process, and to embarrassingly uphold the unreasonable delays. Contrary to common believe, compliance is not a good reason to go slow. Because going slow for safety, is a mistake. It introduces all sorts of disorders, drives down quality, and decelerates even more till a grinding halt. No! Compliance is an excellent motive for better quality and thus to go fast, to produce better, more compliant outcomes.
